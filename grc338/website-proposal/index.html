Introduction

Cafe Borrone

Cafe Borrone is a charmingly welcoming neighborhood caf in the middle of Menlo Park, California, and one of the best places to enjoy a hearty breakfast or brunch. Here they serve fresh, hand-made Belgian waffles, fluffy pancakes, hearty breakfast wraps, and other house-made delicacies every day. The chefs always use first-rate ingredients and prepare everything with meticulous care. Regardless of whether you're in the mood for a leisurely coffee break with a friend, a fun family brunch, or a quick snack on your way to work, Cafe Borrone promises you a cozy, friendly place where you can all gather relax and, of course, savor great food.

Target audience

Mostly local families, student community, working professionals and friend groups form the main clientele of the cafe.

Customers choose the cafe for the tasty meals, relaxed environment and to get a chance to see others around them.
