Welcome to Cafe Borrone

Cafe Borrone is a welcoming neighborhood breakfast cafe in the heart of Menlo Park, California. We serve fresh breakfast and brunch favorites in a warm and inviting atmosphere.

From Belgian waffles and fluffy pancakes to hearty breakfast wraps and handcrafted coffee, every meal is prepared with quality ingredients and care. Whether you are meeting friends, enjoying a family breakfast, or taking a quiet moment for yourself, Cafe Borrone is a comfortable place to gather.

Cafe Borrone • 1010 El Camino Real, Menlo Park, California
